About Me

Hi, I'm Farez Irfan but most of my friends call me Purezz. I'm a freelance photographer who takes photography seriously as a hobby and a part-time job. I started at 16, when I volunteered as a school event photographer and that moment sparked my passion.

I enjoy exploring all types of photography to keep learning, but I'm especially drawn to architecture and buildings. For me, every photo should tell a story not just a picture, but a moment with meaning behind it.
